Stories Of Mumbai

Mumbai the city of dreams has always been one of my most dear cities. The city that inspires
millions of people daily and continues to inspire them forever. I am constantly inspired
by the never give up attitude this city has to offer.The stories the city tells, the people
that make this city have so much to share and inspire the people. This blog shows such stories
that make the city the DREAM'S CITY!






Local Train


Growing up we all have a dream! A dream of becoming someone or meeting some people in our life atleast once!
Our childhood is always made up of dreams and aspirations that we tend to gather for our future selves to be motivated
or be able look back to!
I was just 6 years old when my parents and I along with my younger brother shifted to Mumbai. Mumbai the, city of
dreams! Little did i actually know that this city really had the power and energy to help you come closer to your dreams!
My parents, already familiar with the city, wanted to show me the iconic places and unique things that make the city
more mesmerizing and beautiful!
Local trains being my first beautiful thing to discover in this city! I still remember i was just 7 years old when I had
my first local train journey from vashi to CSMT. I was so curious and nervous at the same time. Hundreds of people
boarded and alighted from the train together! I still wonder how so many people have the same journey but eventually end
up with a different destination and aim in life!
The little girl sat on the window seat, seeing each frame of Mumbai pass by!
I noticed two college going girls standing near the doorway trying to talk to each other amidst the commotion on the train.
The first thing that came to my mind was,
I WANT TO BE LIKE THEM! I didn't know who they were, where they were going but the smiles and courage amidst so much
happening drew me towards them!
I pulled my mother's hand and told her proudly, " Mom, one day I want to be like them and experience life the way they do."
I still have chills when i think of that moment. Two girls inspired me so much! They taught me what the real essence of
Mumbai is! Mumbai taught me to be brave and courageous. It taught me to find joy in the tiniest of things in life!
Who knew life would be a full circle as the girl who once told her mother that she wanted to experience life through
these trains, is actually sitting in one of them and writing this!
